EXAM TITLE: Full NITS Assessment Test
CATEGORY: General

1. If all Bloops are Razzies and all Razzies are Lazzies, then which of the following is definitely true?
A) All Bloops are Lazzies
B) All Lazzies are Bloops
C) All Razzies are Bloops
D) None of the above
Answer: A

2. A train running at 60 km/hr crosses a pole in 9 seconds. What is the length of the train?
A) 120 meters
B) 150 meters
C) 180 meters
D) 200 meters
Answer: B

3. Choose the word most similar in meaning to 'Ephemeral'.
A) Eternal
B) Transient
C) Permanent
D) Durable
Answer: B

4. If the price of a commodity increases by 20%, by what percent must a consumer reduce consumption to maintain the same expenditure?
A) 16.67%
B) 20%
C) 25%
D) 15%
Answer: A

5. In a certain code language, 'COMPUTER' is written as 'RFUVQNPC'. How would 'MEDICINE' be written in that code?
A) ENICFDEM
B) ENIDCFEM
C) FOJEJDNF
D) EOJDCINM
Answer: C

6. A company's revenue was ₹40 Lakhs in Q1, ₹55 Lakhs in Q2, ₹45 Lakhs in Q3, and ₹60 Lakhs in Q4. What was the average quarterly revenue?
A) ₹48 Lakhs
B) ₹50 Lakhs
C) ₹52 Lakhs
D) ₹55 Lakhs
Answer: B

7. Two pipes A and B can fill a tank in 20 minutes and 30 minutes respectively. If both pipes are opened simultaneously, how long will it take to fill the tank?
A) 10 minutes
B) 12 minutes
C) 15 minutes
D) 25 minutes
Answer: B

8. Select the correctly punctuated sentence.
A) Its a beautiful day, isn't it?
B) It's a beautiful day, isn't it?
C) Its a beautiful day isn't it?
D) It's a beautiful day isn't it.
Answer: B

9. If 'A' is the brother of 'B', 'B' is the sister of 'C', and 'C' is the father of 'D', how is 'A' related to 'D'?
A) Uncle
B) Father
C) Grandfather
D) Brother
Answer: A

10. What is the compound interest on ₹10,000 at 10% per annum for 2 years, compounded annually?
A) ₹2,000
B) ₹2,100
C) ₹2,200
D) ₹2,500

A) 38
B) 40
C) 42
D) 44
Answer: C

12. Choose the antonym of 'Benevolent'.
A) Kind
B) Malevolent
C) Generous
D) Charitable

A) 4.33 km/hr
B) 4.5 km/hr
C) 4.67 km/hr
D) 5 km/hr

A) 20%
B) 25%

A) The conclusion is true
B) The conclusion is false
C) The conclusion is uncertain

A) 20 years
B) 24 years

B) Metaphor

A) ₹215
